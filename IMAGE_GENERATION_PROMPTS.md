## Image Specifications

### Recommended Dimensions:
- **Banner/Header Images:** 1920x1080px or 1200x630px (for social media)
- **Documentation Images:** 1600x900px or 1200x800px
- **Architecture Diagrams:** 2000x1500px (landscape, detailed)
- **Infographics:** 1500x2000px (portrait) or 2000x1500px (landscape)

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Dark Blue (#1a1a2e, #16213e)
- **Accent Colors:** 
  - Cyan (#00d4ff) - Normal/Safe
  - Green (#00ff88) - Success
  - Yellow (#ffd700) - Warning
  - Orange (#ff8800) - Suspicious
  - Red (#ff4444) - Critical/Threat

### Style Guidelines:
- Modern, professional, cybersecurity theme
- Clean UI/UX design
- Glowing accents for important elements
- Dark backgrounds with bright accents
- Include RSK World branding
- Technology-focused aesthetic
